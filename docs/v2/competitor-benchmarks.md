## Octopus Energy Germany

**Product:** Intelligent Octopus Go (launched March 2024)

- **Rate:** Maximum 20 ct/kWh during smart charging window (guaranteed cap)
- **Annual savings:** Up to 450 EUR/year vs. standard tariff
- **Cost comparison:** EUR 4 per 100 km (smart EV) vs. EUR 14 per 100 km (petrol)
- **UK benchmark:** 7p/kWh (~8 ct/kWh) smart rate, GBP 543/year savings (Tesla Model 3 example)
- **Requirements:** Smart meter (installed free by Octopus)
- **Tariff:** Fixed price cap on smart window + standard rate for remaining hours, 24-month guarantee

**Relevance:** Closest comparable to our offering. Their 450 EUR/year is achievable with V1G (unidirectional) smart charging alone.

## The Mobility House

**Product:** V2G energy trading on EPEX Spot

- **Berlin field trial (2022):** 18 Audi e-tron batteries, 11 kW each, 1.9 MWh total
- **Result:** "Four-digit EUR gross revenue per vehicle per year"
- **Net to customer:** 650+ EUR/year (after taxes)
- **Markets:** EPEX Spot Day-Ahead + Intraday (direct member, 13 EU countries)
- **THG quota:** Additional 80-200 EUR/year (down from 300 EUR peak in 2022)
- **V2G regulatory milestone:** German Parliament removed V2G barrier in 2025/2026

**Relevance:** Represents the upper bound with V2G hardware. Our V1G-only approach (430 EUR) is lower but requires no special hardware investment.

**Important context:** The 2022 field trial benefited from extreme price volatility (energy crisis). Current spreads are lower but still substantial (130 EUR/MWh average daily spread in 2025).

## 1KOMMA5 (Heartbeat AI)

**Product:** Full energy system (PV + battery + wallbox + heat pump) with AI optimization

- **Case study (Familie Voll):** 2,201 EUR/year total savings
- **EV-specific rate:** 4.76 ct/kWh (grid) vs. 40.92 ct/kWh national average
- **Scale:** 500 MW virtual power plant (Europe's largest)
- **Intraday trading:** Heartbeat AI spun off as separate entity, started intraday optimization
- **Investment:** ~22,000 EUR system cost, ~10 year payback

**Relevance:** Not directly comparable (full system vs. EV-only). But shows the ceiling of what integrated flexibility management can achieve.

---

## Sonnen

**Product:** sonnenFlat + VPP + sonnenHome Charger 2

- **VPP profit:** Up to 100 EUR/year for battery participation in grid services
- **§14a grid incentive:** ~150 EUR/year for controllable wallbox registration
- **Combined:** ~250 EUR/year without V2G
- **Dynamic tariff:** sonnenHome EnergyDynamic (June 2024) claims 30% additional savings

**Relevance:** Lower value (250 EUR) but proven model. Our 430 EUR target exceeds this through more aggressive spot market optimization.

## Market Context

- **BCG Platinion:** 58 billion EUR/year unmonetized flexibility across Europe
- **Only <20% of ~100 GW flexibility capacity is currently monetized**
- **Germany:** ~2.5 million BEVs (growing), smart meter rollout accelerating
- **Mandatory dynamic tariffs since January 2025** — structural enabler
- **15-minute day-ahead products from October 2025** — more optimization granularity
